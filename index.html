The Tweets of OpenVis Conf

Over 3,000 tweets were part of the #openvisconf conversation. Here we look at tweets by and about each speaker from OpenVis 2015.

A timeline of tweets is shown for each speaker. Each bubble is a tweet that is authored by or mentions that speaker. Green bubbles show original tweets. Yellow bubbles show retweets. Original tweets are sized based on the number of retweets they received during the conference. Approximate starting time of the speakers talk is also shown.
